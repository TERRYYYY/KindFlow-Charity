What is your mission?

Our objective is to improve community well-being through a variety of support programs, advocacy, and outreach projects that focus on achieving positive, long-term change.

How can I donate?

Donations are accepted online through our secure website or by mailing a check to our office address. For further information, please visit our Donations page.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, you can subtract your tax contributions from our charity. To assist you with your tax submissions, we will offer you a receipt for your documentation.

Can I volunteer?

Yes, we welcome volunteers! Please visit our Volunteer page to see possibilities and join up for events and activities that align with your interests and availability.

How can I get involved?

To get involved, browse to our Get Involved section for information about volunteering, events, and other ways to help our cause and make a contribution.
